Why do you like learning languages?
I rarely interacted with foreign languages and cultures until I was in my mid-teens. After I moved to Canada, I realized that human languages are so diverse and that knowing them is one of the keys to opening others' minds. It's not easy to acquire foreign languages, but the payoff is tremendous.
