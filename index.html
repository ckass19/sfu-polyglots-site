SFU's Language Club




Who are we?

Established in 2021, SFU Polyglots © is SFU's first and newest language club for students. We are a club for anyone interested in languages and language learning, including non-SFU students. We hold weekly meetings where we play language-related activities and practice languages, as well as workshops, movie nights, and other events!
