The world is precious. It is bursting with beautiful cultures, countries, people, and ways of life. Richard Nicolas

Fluent: English, Tagalog

I find that language is like art. Each language has its own unique structure that connects people. I find that knowing more languages even if you’re not perfect immerses you in its culture. After learning the language, I was able to experience the fullest extent of my culture. I discovered that knowing more languages would give me that benefit of discovering its culture.
